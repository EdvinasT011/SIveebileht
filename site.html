A bit about disc golf

Disc golf is pretty cheap sport regarding equipment, that is being used. You only need a disc and disc golf basket.
There are many disc golf parks in the world including Estonia, which are usually free to use. Rules almost the same
as in regular golf with ball, but with disc. The main goal is to complete the hole in less shots.

Growth of disc golf

Official association of disc golf is called PDGA (Professional Disc Golf Association).
According to their Year End Demographics Report for year 2020, disc golf is growing extremely fast.
17,650 of new PDGA members is increadibly big number for disc golf. In 2019 there was total of 53,366 members
and in the end of 2020 it was 71,016 of active members.

Years: 2018 2019 2020
PDGA active members: 46,457 53,366 71,016
Increase of active members: +5,390 +6,909 +17,650
